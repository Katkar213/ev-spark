About EV Spark:

EV Spark is a pioneering 1D simulation service provider in the eMobility sector. Established in 2018, we are headquartered in Pune and have a branch office in Nagpur. Our expertise lies in the eMobility domain, focusing on electric vehicles (EVs), hybrid electric vehicles (HEVs), and traditional engine development.
